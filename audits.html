FAQ

How long does an audit take?

Express audits with basic checkpoints can be done in a day. But in-depth audits for complex smart contracts can take upto 1-2 weeks.

Who conducts the audits?

Express audits are conducted by a single technical expert, looking for multiple checkpoints. In-depth audits are conducted by a team of blockchain quality analysis experts.

Why do I need a crypto audit?

Investors are much more acquainted with crypto projects these days and will only invest in trusted projects. An audit report and certification helps you enforce trust with your user and mitigate any code vulnerabilities and bugs in smart contracts.
